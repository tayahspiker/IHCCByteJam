ByteJam Rules:


Highschool Division Division

  • No profanity of any kind should be included in a ByteJam project.

  • Students are not allowed to share the ByteJam voting link online.

  • After Lunch Students should throw away their garbage, and not leave them in the bleachers. No Littering.



College Division

  • Students cannot code from previous class projects or personal projects.

  • Students cannot use code found on the internet, learning/researching is okay. And using provided examples or projects is not allowed.

  • Only use approved IDEs and editing software, such as Visual Studio, Visual Studio Code, etc.

  • The project must match the theme, if you have questions about how well your poject fits the theme ask a professor.

  • Coding for ByteJam should never take place outside school hours, coding hours for byte jam are from 8:00 Am to 3:00 Pm

  • Students are still able to reasearch ideas and solutions for their projects outside school hours, but no changes may be made to your project outside school hours.

  • No profanity of any kind should be included in a ByteJam project.

  • Students are not allowed to share the ByteJam voting link online.
