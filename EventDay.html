The Big Event!

Bytejam will be hosted at the Hellyer Student Life Center.


Everyone is expected to arrive and have their tables ready for viewing at 8:00 AM.

  • When the viewing begins, be sure to have at least one person remain at the table to present and explain your teams project.

Companies will be attending, college students should mingle and hand out resumes.


Students can view other projects and vote on them using the vote link above.

  • Students are not permitted to vote for their own teams.

Lunch will be served at 12:00 PM.

  • All students should throw away garbage after they are finished. No Littering.

After Lunch the awards ceremony will begin.

  • Awards will be divided into three different categories: Bits - High School, Bytes - High School, and College. For each category there are three awards: 1st place, 2nd place, and student choice.

The Event is over at 3:00 PM, and clean-up can begin.

  • All students are required to stay until clean-up is finished.
