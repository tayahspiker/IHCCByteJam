Welcome to ByteJam!

    ByteJam is a Computer Software Development Competition hosted by Indian Hills Community College. Everyone will be divided up into different teams made up of only people in the Computer Software Development program. In just three days, your team will make a project relating to that years theme. You and your teammates are only allowed to work on your porject during school hours, 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM!

    Projects that are commonly built are websites, games, and phone apps.

    High school teams are allowed to participate as well! Their teams are judged in two separate divisions, apart from the College division.

    ByteJam is always hosted in November.

    Free lunch is provided to everyone all four days of ByteJam!
